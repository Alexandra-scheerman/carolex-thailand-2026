Before You Go — Pre-Trip Checklist

Vaccinations (visit travel clinic)

  • Must
    Hepatitis A — strongly recommended, via food/water
  • Must
    Typhoid — via food/water, recommended for Thailand
  • Advised
    Hepatitis B — if not already vaccinated (most NL kids are)
  • Advised
    Tetanus/DTP — check if up to date for all 4 of you
  • Optional
    Malaria medication — Khao Sok is geschrapt. Koh Tao/Samui/Phangan zijn laag risico. Vraag reizigersgeneeskundige voor advies — waarschijnlijk niet meer nodig.
  • Optional
    Rabies — for longer jungle stays. Discuss with travel doctor.
  • Optional
    Japanese Encephalitis — very low risk in July, but ask doctor if concerned.

📍 Visit a travel clinic (GGD or Tropeninstitut) at least 6–8 weeks before departure. Bring all 4 passports.

Documents & Visa

  • Must
    Valid passports for all 4 — must be valid 6 months beyond 12 Aug 2026 (= valid until Feb 2027)
  • Must
    Thailand visa — Nederlanders krijgen 30 dagen visumvrij (nieuw per 2026; was 60 dagen). Voor jullie trip van ~20 dagen geen visum nodig. Check actuele regels: thaievisa.go.th
  • Must
    Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) online invullen — voor aankomst online invullen via tdac.immigration.go.th. Voor alle 4 reizigers apart. Doen zodra vlucht geboekt is (max 72u voor aankomst).
  • Must
    Travel insurance for all 4 — including medical evacuation. Check your current coverage.
  • Advised
    Print/save flight e-tickets — especially for kids' flights once booked
  • Advised
    Register trip at BZ.nl — Dutch Foreign Affairs travel registration
  • Advised
    Thai SIM or international data plan — buy SIM at BKK airport on arrival (AIS/DTAC/True Move, ~€15 for 30 days data)

Money & Payments
